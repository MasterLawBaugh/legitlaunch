Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Legit Launch (“we”, “us”, “our”) provides tools to help you protect your brand and intellectual property. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

What we collect

  • Account info: email and password (via Firebase Authentication).
  • App activity: basic usage events (e.g., sign-in status, paywall actions) to operate the app.
  • Purchase info: subscription status via Apple StoreKit. We do not receive your full payment details.
  • Support: messages you send to support@legitlaunch.app.

How we use data

  • Authenticate you and provide app features.
  • Validate subscriptions and access levels.
  • Respond to support requests and improve reliability.

Third-party services

  • Firebase (Authentication, optional Firestore): account and app data storage.
  • Apple StoreKit: manages in-app purchases and subscription status.

Data retention

We retain account and subscription data while your account is active, or as needed for legal/operational purposes. You can request deletion at any time (see “Your rights”).

Your rights

  • Access, update, or delete your account data.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications.
  • Contact us to exercise rights: support@legitlaunch.app.

Children

Legit Launch is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country).

Security

We use industry-standard security practices, but no method is 100% secure.

Changes

We’ll update this policy as needed. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.
